1. Intent

  • Purpose

    • To enable laypersons to visuallt understand the climatic data for a given city, as well as to understand (through visual means), the reasons and the implications of the same.

  • Audience

    • People curious about net-less-than-one efficiency buildings, energy savings as well as more 'natural' lifestyles.

  • Context

    • We rely on architects and engineers to handle the technical aspects of products, with only those that are technically oriented fully understanding teh complexity behind them. This interactive visualization encourages those users to explore the technical aspects in an artistic and intuitive manner.
